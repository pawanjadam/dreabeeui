White-Label Solutions

Empower your business with Dreabee’s white-label solutions, allowing you to leverage our powerful influencer marketing platform under your own brand. With our customizable white-label services, agencies and enterprises can offer their clients a fully branded influencer marketing experience without the need for extensive development.

Why Choose White-Label Solutions?

White-label solutions provide businesses with a fast, cost-effective way to deploy advanced marketing tools under their own branding. By partnering with Dreabee, you gain access to our platform’s features, but with your own logo, color scheme, and brand identity. Ideal for agencies and enterprises looking to expand their service offering with minimal setup.

Customizable Features

Our white-label solution comes with a wide array of customizable features, allowing you to tailor the platform experience to meet your brand’s unique needs. Available customizations include:

  • Customizable branding: Logo, colors, and interface adjustments
  • Personalized domain: Host your platform on a domain of your choice
  • Custom reports: Branded reporting templates for client presentations
  • API integrations: Integrate with your existing systems for seamless data flow

Platform Features with Your Branding

Offer your clients access to Dreabee’s advanced influencer discovery, campaign management, and analytics tools under your brand. With white-label solutions, you’ll have access to:

  • Influencer Search & Audience Demographics
  • Campaign Management & Performance Tracking
  • Comprehensive Analytics Dashboard
  • Customizable Reporting & Insights

Benefits of Dreabee’s White-Label Solutions

Our white-label solutions are designed to help businesses grow and retain clients by providing them with cutting-edge tools and a seamless branded experience. Key benefits include:

  • Fast deployment: Quickly launch a powerful platform under your brand
  • Cost-effective solution: Avoid high development costs
  • Increased client retention: Offer advanced tools that meet client needs
  • Enhanced brand value: Strengthen your brand presence in the market
