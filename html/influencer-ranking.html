How Our Rankings Work

Our influencer rankings are based on a combination of engagement rates, follower growth, content quality, and audience reach. Using Dreabee’s advanced data algorithms, we identify influencers who consistently deliver high engagement and maintain active, genuine audiences.

Metrics used in our ranking include:
Influencer Audience Quality Scores.
Engagement Rate: Average interaction rate on influencer posts.
Follower Growth: Increase in followers over a defined period.
Content Quality: Evaluated based on creativity, consistency, and relevance.
Audience Reach: Total number of people reached through influencer's platform.
